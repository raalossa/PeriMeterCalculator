PERIMETER CALCULATOR

This tool has been made to calulate the longitud of a rolled material. This material is rolled over a core with a diameter. The information we have is:

  • Thickness of the material Example: 500 hundredths of mm
  • External Diameter of the roll, after some rounds over the same material. Example 300 mm
  • internal Diameter over is rolled the material we want to know the longitude Example: 71 mm

As a result the program shows an alert that shows the turns it makes around the initial diameter, and the final perimeter (Longitude).
